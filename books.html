⚡ What to Skip

  • Exam Cram / Official Cert Guides (Network+, Security+, Linux+, PenTest+, CEH, CHFI, SSCP, etc.) — only worth it if you're sitting that specific exam.
  • "For Dummies" titles — too shallow for this path.
  • Overlapping editions — duplicate CEH guides, older Digital Forensics Essentials versions, and similar repeats.

🎯 Reading it straight through

Work through these 43 in order and pair each phase with hands-on labs, and you'll cover networking → Linux → scripting → offense → defense → forensics → OSINT → privacy — and end up over-prepared for OSCP, CEH, or CHFI later, if you decide certifications are worth chasing after all.
